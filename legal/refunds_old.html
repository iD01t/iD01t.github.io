Overview

At iD01t Productions, we are committed to delivering high-quality products and services that meet your expectations. We understand that there may be instances where a product or service does not meet your needs, and we aim to provide a clear and transparent refund policy.

This policy outlines the terms and conditions for requesting refunds on our products and services.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • Technical issues that prevent proper use of the product
  • Product not as described or advertised
  • Duplicate purchases or billing errors
  • Service cancellation within the specified timeframe
  • Defective or non-functional digital products

Digital Products

Software and Apps

Digital software and applications are eligible for refunds within 14 days of purchase if:

  • The software is defective or non-functional
  • Technical issues prevent installation or use
  • The product does not match the description

eBooks and Digital Content

eBooks and digital content may be refunded within 7 days of purchase if:

  • The file is corrupted or unreadable
  • The content is significantly different from what was advertised
  • Technical issues prevent access to the content

Services

For our professional services, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis:

  • Web Development: Refunds available if work has not commenced
  • Consulting: Partial refunds for incomplete sessions
  • Custom Development: Refunds based on project completion status

Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our support team within the refund timeframe
  • Provide your order number and reason for the refund
  • Include any relevant details about the issue
  • Our team will review your request within 2 to 3 business days
  • If approved, refunds are processed within 5 to 10 business days

Exceptions

Refunds may not be available in the following cases:

  • Products used extensively or modified
  • Services already completed and delivered
  • Free or promotional products
  • Violation of our terms of service
  • Requests made outside the refund timeframe

Contact Us

If you have questions about our refund policy or need to request a refund, please contact us:

  • Email: support@id01t.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Us
  • Response Time: 2 to 3 business days
