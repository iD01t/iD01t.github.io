Refund Policy

Clear steps for digital goods, services, and store purchases.

Last updated: September 06, 2025

Table of Contents

1. Overview

We aim for your full satisfaction with our digital products, games, and services. This Refund Policy explains when refunds are available and how to request one.

Last updated: September 06, 2025

2. Refund Eligibility

  • Proof of purchase is required (order number, receipt, or store confirmation).
  • Requests must be submitted within 14 days of purchase unless local law provides longer.

3. Digital Products

For eBooks, downloads, license keys, and other instant digital goods purchased directly from us:

  • Refunds are generally not available once the digital content has been accessed or downloaded.
  • If the file is defective or not as described, we will either replace it or refund you.
  • For purchases made on third-party stores (e.g., itch.io, Amazon, Google), their refund policies apply.

4. Services

For consulting or custom work, refunds are tied to milestone delivery. Deposits may be non‑refundable once work has started, except where required by law.

5. How to Request a Refund

  1. Use the contact page with the subject “Refund Request”.
  2. Include your order details and describe the issue.
  3. We will confirm eligibility and, if approved, issue a refund to the original payment method.

6. Chargebacks

If you dispute a charge with your bank before contacting us, resolution may be delayed. We recommend contacting us first for the fastest outcome.

7. Exceptions

  • Abuse of refund policy, repeated refunds, or suspected fraud.
  • Bundles where only part of the content is returned.
  • Limited-time or beta access keys after activation.

8. Your Rights under Law

Residents of certain jurisdictions may have statutory rights (e.g., EU consumer rights for digital content) which apply in addition to this policy.

9. Contact

Start a request via the contact page.
