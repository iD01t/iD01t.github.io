1. Introduction

iD01t Productions (“we”, “us”, “our”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using our websites, apps, and services, you agree to this Policy.

Last updated: September 06, 2025

2. Who We Are & Data Controller

The data controller is iD01t Productions, based in Canada (Quebec). For all privacy requests, please use the contact page and choose “Privacy.”

3. Information We Collect

  • Account & contact data: name, email, messages you send us.
  • Transactional data: purchase history, downloads, license keys.
  • Usage data: pages viewed, device, approximate region, referral/UTM data.
  • Content you submit: forms, feedback, support tickets.
  • Third-party platform data: if you buy via stores (e.g., itch.io, Amazon, Google), they may share order metadata to fulfill your purchase.

4. Legal Bases (PIPEDA, Quebec Law 25, GDPR)

  • Consent for non-essential cookies, marketing, and optional communications.
  • Contract to deliver purchased digital goods and services.
  • Legitimate interests to secure services, prevent abuse, and improve features.
  • Legal obligations for accounting, taxation, and fraud prevention.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve websites, apps, games, and services.
  • Process orders, deliver downloads, manage licenses, and provide updates.
  • Measure performance and attribution of campaigns (e.g., UTM to itch.io).
  • Communicate about product changes, security alerts, or important notices.

6. Sharing & Processors

We may share data with vetted processors strictly to provide our services (hosting, analytics if enabled, payment processors, customer support). We do not sell personal information.

Third-party embeds (e.g., YouTube, itch.io widgets) may collect data per their own policies.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

8. Security

We apply administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

  • Access, correction, and deletion of your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for optional processing (e.g., marketing, non-essential cookies).
  • Data portability where applicable.
  • Complain to your local authority (e.g., the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada or Quebec CAI).

10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in other countries where our providers operate. Where required, we rely on appropriate transfer safeguards.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child provided data, please contact us.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes to our practices or for legal reasons. We will update the “Last updated” date and, when material, provide a notice.

13. Contact

Use the contact page and select “Privacy.”
