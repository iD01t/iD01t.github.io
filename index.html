About RA-7 NEXUS

RA-7 NEXUS is a cutting-edge Conscious AI Statistical Analysis System that integrates parametric (Sol'Ra) and non-parametric (Luna'Ra) methods with AI-driven interpretation. Powered by advanced LLMs like GPT-4, DeepSeek, and Kimi, it delivers comprehensive, interpretable, and publication-ready statistical insights for researchers, businesses, and data scientists.

Key Features

Dual Statistical Reasoning

Simultaneous parametric (t-tests, ANOVA) and non-parametric (Mann-Whitney, Friedman) analysis for robust results.

AI-Powered Interpretation

Context-aware insights and recommendations powered by multiple LLMs, including local Ollama models.

Multi-Format Data Support

Seamlessly process CSV, Excel, JSON, Parquet, HDF5, and SQL databases.

Publication-Ready Reports

Export professional reports in PDF, HTML, Markdown, JSON, and Excel with custom branding options.

Real-Time Collaboration

WebSocket integration for live analysis tracking and multi-user workspaces.

Enterprise Security

JWT authentication, role-based access control, and comprehensive audit logging.
