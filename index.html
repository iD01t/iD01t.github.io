Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get updates on new products?

You receive free updates via your purchase platform (Google Books, itch.io, Spotify) and our newsletter. We also announce major releases on our social media channels.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer refunds within 14 days for standard products per our refund policy. Digital products are generally non-refundable after download, but we work with customers on a case-by-case basis.

What platforms do you support?

Our apps work on Windows, Mac, and Linux. eBooks are available on Google Books, Kindle, and other major platforms. Music streams on Spotify, YouTube, and Apple Music.

Do you provide technical support?

Yes, we provide email support for all our products. Response time is typically within 24 hours. Check our contact page for support options.

Are your products suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our content ranges from beginner-friendly tutorials to advanced techniques. Each product clearly indicates the skill level required, and we provide learning paths for different experience levels.

Can I use your music in my projects?

Our music is available for personal use and some commercial projects. Please check the specific licensing terms for each track. Contact us for commercial licensing inquiries.
