Official bios

Official bio — English

iD01t Productions is an independent Canadian creative-tech studio founded by Guillaume Lessard and based in Longueuil, Quebec. The studio blends software development, eBook publishing, game design, AI integration, educational technology, digital storytelling, and electronic music into one connected ecosystem. Its public catalog includes 167+ eBooks, 103 audiobooks, Windows utilities, developer tools, indie games, music releases under DJ iD01T, the ROOT_ACCESS / Jess Damaged cyberpunk universe, and the iD01t Research Lab focused on SATI CODEX, LCL frameworks, and quantum error correction. Public platforms include id01t.store, id01t.itch.io, Google Play Books, Apple Music, YouTube, GitHub, LinkedIn, and iTECHINFO.site.

Bio officielle — Français

iD01t Productions est un studio créatif et technologique indépendant canadien fondé en 2023 par Guillaume Lessard et établi à Longueuil (Québec). Le studio fusionne développement logiciel, édition d'eBooks, conception de jeux, intégration de l'IA, technologie éducative, storytelling numérique et musique électronique au sein d'un écosystème connecté : plus de 167 eBooks, 103 audiobooks, des utilitaires Windows et outils développeurs, des jeux indépendants, l'univers cyberpunk ROOT_ACCESS / Jess Damaged, le projet musical DJ iD01T et le iD01t Research Lab (SATI CODEX, frameworks LCL et correction d'erreurs quantiques).

One-liner — English

iD01t Productions — an independent creative-tech studio from Longueuil, Québec, shipping software, books, games, music and quantum research since 2023.

Founder bio — Guillaume Lessard

Guillaume Lessard is a Canadian developer, author, music producer and independent researcher. He is the founder of iD01t Productions, records as DJ iD01T, writes the Jess Damaged Archives, and directs the iD01t Research Lab (ORCID 0009-0000-3465-3753). He also runs iTECHINFO, an IT and mobile software service in Montréal.
