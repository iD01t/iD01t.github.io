About the Story

A golden medallion wakes inside an ancient temple. Three curious kittens follow its light through a living labyrinth to meet El’Nox Rah, a guardian who speaks in riddles and star‑maps. Adventure, wonder, and gentle suspense—designed for kids, made for families.
