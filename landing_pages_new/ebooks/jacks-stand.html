Jack’s Stand

Jack’s Stand: An Environmental Lawyer’s Fight for Justice (Edition 2)

By Guillaume Lessard

A poisoned river, a shattered community, and a corporation that will stop at nothing to silence the truth.

Jack Hawthorne, a relentless environmental lawyer, has dedicated his life to taking down Evergreen Industries—the corporate titan responsible for devastating Riverview’s water and destroying countless lives. When Olivia Bennett, a fearless investigative journalist, uncovers a trail of offshore accounts, shell companies, and secret projects, their worlds collide in a battle for justice.

What begins as a legal fight quickly spirals into a deadly conspiracy. Whistleblowers vanish, encrypted memos surface, and powerful figures will do anything to protect their empire. From midnight meetings at shadowy piers to high-stakes courtroom maneuvers, Jack and Olivia must navigate a web of lies, betrayal, and intimidation. Every step forward brings them closer to the trut, and deeper into danger.

Edition 2 is fully expanded with richer character development, heightened suspense, and new revelations, including the explosive chapter Into the Crosshairs. This definitive edition transforms Jack’s Stand into a cinematic legal thriller packed with corporate intrigue, moral dilemmas, and a relentless pursuit of justice.

Perfect for fans of John Grisham, Michael Connelly, and political thrillers, Jack’s Stand (Edition 2) will keep you turning pages late into the night, questioning how far you would go to expose the truth.
