Announcing HTML2exe Pro

Published on September 8, 2025

We are excited to announce the launch of HTML2exe Pro, a powerful tool for developers and creators who want to turn their HTML projects into professional Windows applications.

HTML2exe Pro is a desktop application builder with a modern React-style GUI, PyInstaller integration, icon generation, CSP, sandboxing, templates, and live preview. It's the perfect tool for creating and distributing your HTML-based projects as standalone Windows applications.

You can learn more about HTML2exe Pro on our Apps page.
