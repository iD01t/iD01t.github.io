About iD01t Productions

A Canadian creative technology studio uniting software engineering, AI, music, and publishing to empower creators worldwide

Our Story Get in Touch

Innovation at the Intersection

iD01t Productions is a Canadian creative and technology studio founded by Guillaume Lessard. We unite software engineering, artificial intelligence, music, and publishing into a single ecosystem designed to empower creators worldwide.

From desktop apps that simplify workflows to technical eBooks that train the next generation of developers, from independent games that blend humor and intensity to music projects that inspire audiences across platforms, iD01t Productions thrives at the intersection of technology and creativity.

Our philosophy is simple: automation first, clarity always, and freedom for creators. We focus on building products that save time, maximize return on investment, and provide tangible value. Every release, whether an app, a book, or an album, is shipped with professional quality and a commitment to innovation.

What We Do

Our diverse portfolio spans multiple industries, all unified by our commitment to excellence and innovation

Software Development

Development of Windows apps and utilities for creators and businesses, integrating AWS and advanced AI for scalability

Publishing

Publication of eBooks and audiobooks on programming, AI, design, and personal growth, including our 50-book love story saga

Game Development

Production of independent video games with multiplayer and cross-platform reach, creating universes that expand into literature and music

Music Production

Release of original electronic music under the alias DJ iD01t, reaching listeners across Spotify, Apple Music, and major platforms

AI Integration

Integration of AWS and advanced AI into every project for scalability, automation, and enhanced user experiences

Creative Projects

Long-form creative projects including educational series for developers and interconnected universes spanning multiple media

Our Vision

Based in Montreal, iD01t Productions operates globally with a digital-first approach, reaching readers, players, and listeners across Amazon, Apple, Google, Spotify, Deezer, itch.io, and beyond. Our vision is to inspire bold thinking, build communities, and design tools that empower creators to take control of their craft.
