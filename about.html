// about

One studio, five verticals, one ecosystem

Where creativity meets technology. iD01t Productions is an independent creative-tech studio founded in 2023 in Longueuil, Québec by Guillaume Lessard — and run solo, end to end.

The story

Positioned in the software development sector, the studio publishes technical and creative eBooks and audiobooks, builds Windows applications and developer tools, develops indie games, produces electronic music as DJ iD01T, and conducts public research in quantum error correction through the iD01t Research Lab.

The ROOT_ACCESS / Jess Damaged transmedia universe ties cyberpunk fiction, industrial albums, games and digital assets into a single narrative continuity — from the streets of NeoShanghai to the servers of OmniCore.

The studio's motto says the rest: « We ship. Even tired. Even messy. Still shipping. »
