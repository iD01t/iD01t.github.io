About

iD01t Productions is a Canadian creative and technology studio founded by Guillaume Lessard. It unites software engineering, artificial intelligence, music, and publishing into a single ecosystem designed to empower creators worldwide.

From desktop apps that simplify workflows to technical eBooks that train the next generation of developers, from independent games that blend humor and intensity to music projects that inspire audiences across platforms, iD01t Productions thrives at the intersection of technology and creativity.

Our philosophy is simple: automation first, clarity always, and freedom for creators. We focus on building products that save time, maximize return on investment, and provide tangible value. Every release, whether an app, a book, or an album, is shipped with professional quality and a commitment to innovation.

Key activities include:

  • Development of Windows apps and utilities for creators and businesses
  • Publication of eBooks on programming, AI, design, and personal growth
  • Production of independent video games with multiplayer and cross‑platform reach
  • Release of original electronic music under the alias DJ iD01t
  • Integration of AWS and advanced AI into every project for scalability

The studio also manages long‑form creative projects, including a 50‑book love story saga, multiple educational series for developers, and game universes that expand into literature and music. Everything produced carries the brand values of innovation, independence, and excellence.

Based in Montreal, iD01t Productions operates globally with a digital‑first approach, reaching readers, players, and listeners across Amazon, Apple, Google, Spotify, Deezer, itch.io, and beyond. Our vision is to inspire bold thinking, build communities, and design tools that empower creators to take control of their craft.
