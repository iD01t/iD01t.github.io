Light at the Veil’s Edge

ight at the Veil’s Edge: The Hidden Flame of Kirlian Photography

By Guillaume Lessard (El’Nox)

What if your soul left fingerprints in light?

Light at the Veil’s Edge invites you into the mysterious world of Kirlian photography, where fingertips flare like auroras, leaves whisper in luminous flame, and energy fields reveal hidden truths about life itself. Written by mystic author Guillaume Lessard, also known as El’Nox, this book is a poetic and scientific exploration of the space where voltage meets spirit.

Split into four immersive parts—The Veil and the Vision, Light as Language, The Mystic’s Lens, and Practice and Pilgrimage—this guide illuminates both the metaphysical and scientific dimensions of biofields, aura interpretation, and the visible song of the soul.

Inside you will discover:

✅ The history, science, and mysticism of Kirlian imaging

✅ How emotions, health, and intention shape the biofield glow

✅ The practice of reading images as soul signatures

✅ Connections between ancient spiritual light traditions and modern corona discharge photography

✅ Practical steps for analog and digital Kirlian setups

✅ Guided journaling, energy healing techniques, and emerging spiritual technologies

Rich with reflections, case studies, and hands-on methods, Light at the Veil’s Edge is more than a book. It is a sacred mirror that bridges mysticism and science while offering readers a chance to witness their own radiance.

Whether you are a curious skeptic, a spiritual seeker, an energy healer, or a modern mystic, this book will expand your perception of what it means to be alive, present, and luminous.

You are not just visible. You are radiant. And the veil has opened.
