Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you typically work on?

I specialize in automation systems, cross-platform applications, AI integration, and strategic technology consulting. My focus is on building solutions that generate revenue, scale across platforms, and create technological independence for businesses and individuals.

What's your typical project timeline and process?

Projects typically range from 4-16 weeks depending on complexity. My process includes: 1) Strategic consultation and requirements analysis, 2) Architecture design and planning, 3) Iterative development with weekly check-ins, 4) Testing and optimization, 5) Deployment and training, 6) Post-launch support and optimization.

Do you work with startups or only established companies?

I work with both startups and established companies. For startups, I offer equity-based partnerships and phased development approaches. For enterprises, I provide comprehensive automation and strategic technology solutions. Every project is evaluated based on potential impact and strategic alignment.

What technologies and platforms do you work with?

Core technologies include Python, C#, Unity, React, Node.js, and AI/ML frameworks. I build for Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android. Cloud platforms include AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure. I prioritize cross-platform compatibility and self-hostable solutions for maximum independence.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, I offer comprehensive support packages including monitoring, updates, scaling assistance, and feature enhancements. I believe in building long-term relationships and ensuring your systems continue to provide value as your needs evolve.

How do you ensure project success and ROI?

Every project includes clear success metrics, ROI tracking, and monetization strategies. I focus on building systems that pay for themselves through automation savings, revenue generation, or operational efficiency. Regular milestone reviews ensure we stay aligned with your business objectives.
