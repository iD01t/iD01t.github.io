Nini's Adventures: Kitties Mayhem!

Release Date: In 20 days

Nini's Adventures: Kitties Mayhem! is not just another online game. It is a full scale chaotic multiplayer experience where cute kitties transform into fierce warriors in vibrant and unpredictable arenas. Explosions, power ups, and mayhem await every time you log in.

Whether you want to test your reflexes, team up with friends, or prove you are the ultimate kitty champion, every battle is designed to keep the energy high and the fun unstoppable.

Multiplayer FPS Arena 3D Fast-Paced Cats
Play Beta on itch.io

Key Features

  • Competitive online multiplayer with the first public beta opening on September 24, 2025
  • Chaotic cat themed arenas filled with traps, obstacles, and surprises for maximum mayhem
  • Fast responsive gameplay designed for quick reflexes and intense matches
  • AWS powered servers ensuring stability, scalability, and smooth global play
  • Unique kitty characters with special abilities that bring variety and strategy to every match
  • Bright colorful visuals with destructible environments that react to the action

Beta Information

This is the very first PC beta release. There is no single player mode. There is no console or mobile support in this stage. This phase focuses only on online competitive battles to test servers, gameplay balance, and player feedback.

Players who subscribe with their email will secure exclusive access to the September 24, 2025 public beta.
